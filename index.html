"I built With Light because I believe every person deserves support that sees them as a whole human being — not just a participant number."

Nour Ladkani — Director & Social Worker

With Light was founded by Nour Ladkani, a qualified Social Worker with deep roots in the South West Sydney community. We put clinical expertise at the center of every support we deliver.

We specialize in supporting people with psychosocial disabilities with the kind of nuanced, recovery-oriented care that makes a real difference.

We are proudly Arabic-speaking and culturally safe for the diverse families of Liverpool and Leppington.
