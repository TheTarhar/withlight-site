Our Story

Social Work at the
heart of everything.

"I built With Light because I believe every person deserves support that sees them as a whole human being — not just a participant number."

Nour Ladkani — Director & Social Worker, With Light

With Light was founded by Nour Ladkani, a qualified Social Worker with deep roots in the Liverpool and South West Sydney community. Unlike many NDIS providers, we put clinical expertise at the centre of every support we deliver.

We specialise in supporting people with psychosocial disabilities — mental health conditions, complex trauma, and dual diagnoses — with the kind of nuanced, recovery-oriented care that makes a real difference.

We are proudly Arabic-speaking and culturally safe for the diverse communities of South West Sydney, including CALD families who have historically been underserved by mainstream disability services.

Platform Features

Built for NDIS excellence.

Our platform brings together everything an NDIS provider needs — compliance, clinical documentation, and family visibility — in one place.

📋

D.A.P. Clinical Notes

Every shift note follows the Data, Assessment, Plan clinical format — ensuring quality documentation that meets NDIS audit standards.

📍

GPS Verification

Clock-in is GPS verified at the participant's address, protecting against ghost shifting and giving families peace of mind.

🚦

Compliance Monitoring

Traffic light system tracks every worker's certifications — WWCC, Police Check, NDIS Screening — with automatic alerts before expiry.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Portal

Families can see upcoming shifts, goal progress, and shift summaries in real time, keeping them connected to their loved one's care.

🤝

Coordinator Portal

Support Coordinators can refer participants, track onboarding status, and monitor goal progress — making With Light their easiest referral.

🌐

Arabic & English

The entire platform is available in both English and Arabic, making it accessible to the CALD communities we proudly serve.

Why We Built With Light
As NDIS providers ourselves, we saw a gap between complex, clunky software and the actual needs of families in South West Sydney. We didn't just want a database; we wanted a bridge. With Light was born from a need for bilingual transparency, GPS-verified safety, and a simple 'Traffic Light' system that keeps compliance from being a headache. We built it to support our team, and now we've built it to support yours.
