About With Light

Personalised care
with clinical depth.

With Light is an NDIS provider rooted in Social Work values — where every decision is guided by a qualified social worker who understands the full context of a person's life, not just their support plan.

We specialise in psychosocial disability: the intersection of mental health, day-to-day function, and community connection. Our approach is trauma-informed, strengths-based, and deeply respectful of each person's cultural identity.

Based in South West Sydney — Liverpool, Fairfield, Bankstown, Campbelltown — we bring culturally safe, bilingual support directly into the communities we serve.

✦ Psychosocial Disability ✦ Trauma-Informed ✦ Culturally Safe ✦ NDIS Registered

Platform Features

Built for the real work
of NDIS support.

Our platform was designed by support workers, for support workers — with the clinical oversight that families and coordinators can trust.

📝

Clinical D.A.P Reporting

Structured Data, Action, Plan notes submitted digitally after every shift. Each note is reviewed within our social work quality framework — ensuring meaningful, auditable records.

✦ NDIS Audit Ready
📍

Real-Time GPS Logs

Automatic shift start and end verification via GPS. Families and coordinators get transparent, timestamped location records — building trust through verified accountability.

✦ Live Verification
🌐

Multi-Language Portal

The participant portal and all communications are available in English, Arabic, Vietnamese, Hindi, Assyrian, and Cantonese — removing language as a barrier to understanding your own plan.

✦ 6 Languages
🚦

Traffic Light Compliance

A simple green–amber–red system tracks worker document compliance in real time. Expiring certifications surface automatically — no manual chasing required.

✦ Automated Alerts
👨‍👩‍👧

Family Transparency Dashboard

Families can securely view shift summaries, notes, and GPS check-ins through a dedicated portal — fostering confidence and open communication at every stage of care.

✦ Real-Time Access

Why We Built With Light
As NDIS providers ourselves, we saw a gap between complex, clunky software and the actual needs of families in South West Sydney. We didn't just want a database; we wanted a bridge. With Light was born from a need for bilingual transparency, GPS-verified safety, and a simple 'Traffic Light' system that keeps compliance from becoming a headache. We built it to support our team — and now we've built it to support yours.
